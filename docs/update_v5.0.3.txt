Hyperbot V5.0.3: The Kinetic Baseline & Entry Expansion

Objective: Rectify a critical mathematical hallucination in the baseline order flow calculation that caused artificial "division-by-zero" volume spikes. Expand the execution cages (VWAP and CVD) to capture realistic micro-squeezes while leaving the ATR capital preservation armor absolutely untouched.

1. The Baseline Reality Correction (Total Kinetic Energy)

Target File: multi_sim.js (or relevant feed/state logic)

Target Function: updateCVDBaseline()

The Flaw: The previous version calculated avgRate using the net difference of buyers and sellers (Math.abs(buyVolume - sellVolume) / 60). In a perfectly balanced market, this creates a near-zero denominator. A tiny 1 BTC market order would then divide by zero, register as a false "15x spike", and trigger a bad trade.

The Fix: We must measure the total kinetic energy (background noise) of the room, not the net delta.

Execution: Update the baseline calculation to use the total volume:

// Measure total kinetic energy of the market, not net delta.
const totalVol = buyVolume + sellVolume;
this.cvdBaseline.avgRate = totalVol > 0 ? (totalVol) / 60 : 0;


(Note: The 5-second directional spike calculation remains untouched. We still divide the 5-second directional net-delta by this new 60-second total-volume baseline).

2. Expanding the Entry Cages

Target File: multi_sim.js (Constants Configuration)

The Flaw: The VWAP proximity (0.1%) and the CVD Spike Threshold (2.0x) were suffocatingly tight. The bot demanded perfectly centered price action combined with apocalyptic volume spikes.

The Fix: 1.  Widen VWAP: Change VWAP_PROXIMITY_PCT from 0.001 to 0.002. This allows the bot a 0.2% variance to capture heavy volume that is "front-running" the VWAP line for a bounce or rejection.
2.  Lower CVD Threshold: Change CVD_SPIKE_THRESHOLD from 2.0 to 1.5. A 1.5x surge in directional volume relative to total background noise is plenty of kinetic energy to confirm a micro-squeeze.

3. The Capital Preservation Armor (DO NOT MODIFY)

The Volatility Hurdle: The volMultiplier sweep (0.0007 to 0.0010, targeting 0.0008) MUST remain active. The entry must mathematically clear the taker fees based on the 1-minute ATR. Do not loosen this.

The 60-Second Scratch Rule: The MOMENTUM_INVALIDATION rule implemented in V5.0.2 remains untouched. If a trade has not cleared fees and is underwater at 60 seconds, it exits via Post-Only Limit order.
