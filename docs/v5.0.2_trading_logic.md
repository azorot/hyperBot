# Hyperbot V5.0.2 Trading and Entry Logic

This document details the trading, entry, and exit logic implemented in the Hyperbot multi-simulator.

---

## 1. Entry Logic
Entry decisions are evaluated inside the `onTick` method of each `StrategyInstance` and triggered through `checkVWAPEntry`. An entry is triggered when the strategy is flat (`position.size === 0`) and the following confluence conditions are met:

### A. Baseline Filters
*   **Volume Filter (`hasMinVolume`)**: The rolling 60-second volume (buy + sell) must exceed the minimum threshold of **10.0 BTC** (`MIN_VOLUME_THRESHOLD`).
*   **Cooldown Check**: The direction (LONG/SHORT) must not be cooling down from a recent stop-loss hit.

### B. Confluence of Signals
1.  **VWAP Proximity**: The current mid price must be within **0.1%** (`VWAP_PROXIMITY_PCT = 0.001`) of the VWAP:
    *   Price **above** VWAP: Potential **LONG** bounce.
    *   Price **below** VWAP: Potential **SHORT** rejection.
2.  **Volatility Hurdle**: The fast ATR (hourly) must be greater than the price multiplied by the instance's specific `volMultiplier` (e.g., `0.0007` to `0.0010`):
    $$\text{ATR} > \text{midPrice} \times \text{volMultiplier}$$
    If volatility is too dry, the setup is bypassed.
3.  **CVD Order Flow Spike (`getCVDSpike`)**: 
    *   In a 5-second window, the Cumulative Volume Delta (CVD) rate (BTC/sec) must be at least **2x** the baseline 60-second average CVD rate (`CVD_SPIKE_THRESHOLD = 2.0`).
    *   The spike direction must match the trade bias (positive CVD for LONG, negative CVD for SHORT).
4.  **Price Momentum Confirmation (`getSpikeDelta`)**: The price direction within the same 5-second spike window must confirm the direction (increasing price for LONG, decreasing price for SHORT). This prevents taking trades where CVD spikes but price does not move (absorbing/trap liquidity).

### C. Sizing and Stops Setup
*   **Risk Capping (`calculatePositionSize`)**: Capped at **2% equity** risk per trade (`RISK_PER_TRADE_PCT`) with a maximum hard leverage cap of **3x** (`MAX_LEVERAGE`).
*   **Hard Stop**: Placed at $1.5 \times \text{ATR}$ (or a maximum cap of **0.5%** of the entry price).
*   **Adaptive Initial Trailing Callback**: Calculated dynamically based on the current relative ATR:
    $$\text{Initial Stop} = \text{volMultiplier} \times \frac{\text{Relative ATR}}{\text{Baseline Relative ATR (0.05\%)}}$$
    This is bounded between **0.02%** and **0.50%**.

## 2. Trading (Exit) Logic
Exits are evaluated on every tick when a position is open (`position.size !== 0`) inside `checkMicroSqueezeExit` and `checkFundingIntercept`. 

There are 5 different exit mechanisms:

### A. 15-Minute Kill Switch (Timeout)
*   If the trade is open for $\ge 15$ minutes (`900,000 ms`), the position is closed immediately using a **Post-Only Limit Order** at the spread (`TIMEOUT_15M`) to avoid paying taker fees.

### B. Hard Stop-Loss
*   If the price breaches the hard stop-loss (`dynamicHardStop`), a **Taker Market Order** is executed immediately (`HARD_STOP`).
*   Both LONG and SHORT sides are put on a **5-minute cooldown** (`COOLDOWN_MS`).

### C. 60-Second Momentum Invalidation (Scratch Trade)
*   If the trade is open for $\ge 60$ seconds, has **not** cleared the taker fee threshold (`clearedFees` is false), and is **underwater** (unrealized PnL < 0), the setup is considered failed.
*   The strategy exits via a **Post-Only Maker Limit Order** at the spread to preserve capital and avoid paying taker fees on a dead trade (`MOMENTUM_INVALIDATION`).

### D. Time-Decay Trailing Stop
*   **Activation**: The trailing stop only starts updating once the price moves enough to clear taker fee friction (`clearedFees` is true).
*   **Linear Decay**: To protect accrued profits as time passes, the trailing stop callback percentage decays linearly starting from 1 minute to 10 minutes:
    *   **Time < 1 min**: Trailing callback is equal to the dynamic initial stop.
    *   **Time between 1 min and 10 mins**: Callback decays linearly down to a floor of **0.02%**.
    *   **Time > 10 mins**: Callback stays pinned at **0.02%** (ultra-tight trail).
*   If the trailing stop is breached, a **Post-Only Limit Order** is placed to exit (`TRAIL_EXIT`).

### E. Funding Fee Intercept (`checkFundingIntercept`)
*   Checked between minutes 50 and 55 of the hour (right before funding payment).
*   If the funding rate is adverse (e.g., paying funding fees for holding this position), the trailing stop callback is tightened to a flat **0.05%** (`TRAIL_CB_FUNDING_BAD = 0.0005`) to lock in profits or scratch the trade before the fee hits.
